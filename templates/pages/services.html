What We Build

Our Services

From single Minars to complete mosque complexes — every component built with masterful precision and deep reverence.

01
Minar Construction
Octagonal, cylindrical, tapered or fluted minars with ornate balconies, geometric bands, and crescent finials. Heights 20ft–200ft.
02
Dome Fabrication
Onion domes to ribbed Ottoman and Persian domes — in RCC, steel or fiberglass, finished with mosaic tiles, copper or marble cladding.
03
Mosque Construction
Complete mosque construction from architectural design to calligraphic details. Prayer halls, wudu areas, minarets, and domes included.
04
Madarsa Buildings
Purpose-built Islamic educational institutions with traditional arched corridors, central courtyards, study halls, and residential blocks.
05
Renovation & Restoration
Breathing new life into heritage mosques — structural restoration, dome repair, minar reinforcement, calligraphy revival and upgrades.
06
Islamic Ornamentation
Geometric Arabesques, Muqarnas, Zellij tile mosaic, calligraphic panels, jali screens, and hand-carved plasterwork — art in every surface.

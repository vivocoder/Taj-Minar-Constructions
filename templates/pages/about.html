Who We Are

Masters of Islamic Architecture

Al-Emaar Constructions is a premier firm dedicated exclusively to sacred Islamic architecture. For over two decades, we have transformed visions into magnificent structures that stand as testaments to faith and craftsmanship.

Founded in 2005 in Lahore, we began with a singular mission: to elevate the quality of Islamic construction in Pakistan. Today, we have completed 150+ projects across 12 cities, becoming the most trusted name in mosque, minar, and dome construction.

Every Minar we erect, every Dome we craft — done with reverence, precision, and deep understanding of Islamic architectural heritage from Mughal to Ottoman traditions.
